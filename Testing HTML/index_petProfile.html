Pritorize the animals who need us MOST

Here at Paw Patrol, we are focused on the animals who have been in the shelter the longest. As animals spend an increasingly long time in the shelters, they are less likely to be adopted which results in unjust euthanization. With Paw Patrol, these overlooked animals will finally be able to get the priority and emphasis they need and deserve. We aim to help the animals in need, but we need your help.
